Our Story

Built on trust, one tool at a time.

Industrial Powertool started with a simple idea: Richmond's industry deserves a supplier where the person behind the counter has actually run the equipment on the shelf. We're family-owned and independent — which means our only loyalty is to the customer walking in the door. If a cheaper tool does the job, we'll tell you. If it won't survive your work, we'll tell you that too.
